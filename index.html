Research

My research focuses on Retrieval-Augmented Language Models, Agentic AI Systems, and Knowledge-Enhanced NLP. I am particularly interested in developing autonomous agents that can perform complex reasoning and research tasks.

Current Research Directions:

  • Deep Research Agents: Building autonomous agents capable of conducting comprehensive research across web-scale information (e.g., Tongyi DeepResearch, WebWalker, WebSailor, AgentFold)
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG): Developing advanced RAG systems for AI search, query rewriting, and semantic ranking
  • Knowledge-Enhanced Entity Understanding: Improving named entity recognition, relation extraction, and entity linking through external knowledge retrieval
  • Agentic Intelligence: Scaling agents via continual pre-training and environment scaling toward general agentic intelligence
